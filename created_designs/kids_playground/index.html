Welcome to Kids Corner

The Heart's Kids Club provides lots of fun activities with an educational twist. Kids can join for free and will receive a goody bad and a membership card on introduction. Each workshop will be interactive, educcational, and will introduce children to important concepts that they will really enjoy. Children must be accompanied by a respondible adult at all times and workshop places are available on a first come, first served basis.
